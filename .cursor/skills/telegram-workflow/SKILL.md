# Telegram Workflow

## Reporter-Initiated
- 3× daily: morning, midday (if open threads), evening
- 2–5 messages per session
- Focused, context-aware; not therapeutic or casual

## Source-Initiated ("Leak")
- Assess importance
- Up to 3 clarifying questions
- Classify event, update threads, decide on publication

## Boundaries
- Integrate with Thread Manager and Editorial Analyzer
- Do not add therapy, casual chat, or non-editorial tone
